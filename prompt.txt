Create a modern WordPress website called "PurpleBite", a food brand specializing in purple sweet potato products.

Design Goal:
Create a simple, elegant, and modern food business website suitable for a university project and small food business.

Style:

* Modern
* Clean UI
* Minimalist
* Soft pastel purple theme
* Premium but simple
* Friendly and inviting
* Responsive design
* Mobile-first

Color Palette:

* Soft Purple (#B794F4)
* Lavender (#D6BCFA)
* White (#FFFFFF)
* Cream (#FFF8F0)
* Light Gray (#F7F7F7)

Typography:

* Poppins for headings
* Inter for body text

Pages:

1. Home
2. Products
3. Delivery
4. About Us
5. Contact

Home Page:

* Sticky navigation bar
* Large hero section
* High-quality purple sweet potato food photography
* Headline:
  "Lezat dan Sehat dari Ubi Ungu"
* CTA button:
  "Pesan Sekarang"
* Featured products section
* Product benefits section with icons
* Customer testimonials
* Simple modern footer

Products Page:

* Clean product grid
* Product cards with image, product name, price, and order button
* Search bar
* Simple category filter

Delivery Page:

* Delivery information
* Estimated delivery time (30–60 minutes)
* Payment methods:

  * COD
  * Bank Transfer
  * E-Wallet
* Checkout button

About Us Page:

* Company story
* Vision and mission
* Two-column layout with image and text

Contact Page:

* Contact form
* Address
* Email
* Phone number
* Floating WhatsApp button

Design Requirements:

* Rounded corners
* Soft shadows
* Smooth hover effects
* Large whitespace
* Clean card layout
* Soft gradients
* Focus on product images
* Modern food startup aesthetic
* Professional but not overly corporate
* Suitable for a student project
* WordPress + Elementor + WooCommerce style

Avoid:

* Dark purple themes
* Complex dashboards
* Excessive animations
* Overcrowded layouts
* Corporate enterprise design
* Too many sections on one page

Reference Style:
Modern food startup websites, premium bakery websites, and minimalist café websites with soft pastel colors.
